THE SHERRIF'S LIST
THE AMERICAN IDOL

Huzaifa, the one they call the "American Psycho," is a feller straight outta the New World, speakin’ with a twang as sharp as his rizzifying poses. Known for his bold actions and a gaze that could freeze a rattlesnake, he’s got a way o’ makin’ folks stop and take notice. With a flair for the dramatic and a confidence that don’t quit, Huzaifa’s the kinda man who walks into a room and leaves a legend behind.

SHELBY

Moiz, known far and wide as "Shelby," carries himself with the cool swagger of a lone gunslinger and the rizz of a high-stakes poker player. A true Sugma male, his nonchalant ways and sharp wit have earned him a reputation as a man who don’t flinch in the face of trouble. With charisma that could tame a wild stallion, Moiz commands respect without ever raisin’ his voice—a natural-born leader of his own makin’.

THE TOPG

Musa, the ex-CR, is a man o’ grit and charm, built like a frontier blacksmith and smooth as a silver-tongued gambler. With the muscle to move mountains and the know-how to lead a rowdy posse, his time as CR left a mark like boots in the desert dust. A true gunslinger of leadership, he’s got the past to prove his worth.
