THE WANTED LIST
SIGMA BABE

AFAQ, the sigma baby of Classroom Gulch, strides through the day like a lone wolf with a knack for mischief. The pookie and comedian rolled into one, he’s quick with a quip and sharper than a cactus needle, leaving classmates roaring with laughter and teachers fighting back smirks. A true wildcard, he’s the laughter in the dusty halls.

POOKIE

Zamin, the feller with an aura brighter than a desert sunrise, moseys into town with General Rizla, his sharp-eyed hound, and his stubborn ol’ ass. A true pookie, he’s got antics wilder than a stampede and a knack for dramatics that’d leave the saloon hollerin’ and hootin’.

THE MACHO MAN

Mahad, the Macho Man of the Classroom Range, reigns as the high-falutin’ admin of the study gang. With a jawline sharper than a Bowie knife and a gaze that cuts like prairie wind, he’s a sight to behold. Beneath his quiet hide lies a sleeper build, strong as a bronco and steady as a frontier oak. Mahad don’t just walk the range—he owns it, the Macho Man in all his rugged glory.
