AI—C CR—SHIP History
In the dusty annals of AI-C’s tumultuous history, the tale of its CR-ship reads like a chapter straight out of the Wild West. The dawn of this saga saw Musa, a bold gunslinger dubbed the “TopG,” handpicked by the class sheriff, Madam Shumaila, to lead the unruly posse. But by the second week, the saloon was abuzz with whispers, and a minor misunderstanding brewed between the boys and girls of the town, a storm that no leader could tame. Disheartened, Musa hung up his badge, leaving the post vacant. Into this void rode Muzzamil Yasin, a steady hand to wrangle the wayward AI-C gang. Yet, the winds of change are stirring once more, and it seems the time has come for a new lawmaker to step into the spotlight and steer this rowdy town into its next chapter.
